The Drummond Lab

We study how cells adapt at the molecular level, focusing on translation, the process by which cells produce proteins using genetic information. Our analyses extend from the near-instantaneous cellular responses to environmental stress to the multi-million-year evolutionary divergence of species.

Our overarching strategy is to build and integrate biochemical, biophysical, cell-biological, and evolutionary data into a coherent whole. We test and exploit useful findings by engineering molecules using our biological discoveries.
