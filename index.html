The Drummond Lab

We study how cells adapt at the molecular level, focusing on translation, the production of proteins from genetic information. Our analyses extend from the near-instantaneous cellular responses to environmental stress to the multi-million-year evolutionary divergence of species.

Our overarching strategy is to build and integrate biochemical, biophysical, cell-biological, and evolutionary data into a coherent whole.
